# Product Guidelines

## Prose and Voice
Our tone is **Friendly & Inspirational**. We want to welcome travelers and motivate them with evocative language that paints a picture of their dream vacation, while remaining accessible and helpful.

## Branding and Visuals
The visual direction is **Minimalist & Modern**. We leverage the clean aesthetics of Tailwind CSS and `shadcn/ui` to create a focused, high-performance interface that lets travel imagery and content take center stage without unnecessary clutter.

## User Experience (UX)
We adhere to a **Mobile-First Focus**. All features and layouts are designed and optimized for smartphone users first, ensuring a fast and seamless experience on the go before scaling to larger displays.

## Error Handling and Communication
Our error communication is **Direct & Instructional**. When something goes wrong—whether it's a search failure or a booking error—we tell the user clearly what happened and provide specific steps to resolve the issue immediately.
